FAQ

Questions before you start designing?

Keep purchase concerns close to reviews: customization preview, production timing, image quality and gift-ready options should be answered before asking for an email signup.

Visit help center
Can I preview my personalized gift before it is made?

Yes. Products with Start Design open a customization panel where customers can choose color, flower, name and quantity before adding the gift to cart.

How long does production usually take?

Most personalized gifts need a short production window before shipping. Use the product detail page and order timeline to show the latest estimated production and delivery dates.

What kind of photo or name works best?

Use clear, high-resolution photos and short names when possible. The design card should show character limits, required fields and preview details before checkout.

Can I add a gift box or message?

Gift box, note and packaging options should appear inside the customization or checkout flow when available, with required fields marked by a star instead of long labels.

What happens if I need to change an order?

Customers should contact support quickly with their order number. Once production starts, some personalized details may no longer be editable.
